About Madeira

Madeira is a Portuguese island in the Atlantic Ocean about 500 km from the mainland. The climate is fantastic and the temperature ranges from 25 to 30 degrees in summer to 17 degrees in winter. It has many different landscapes with dense forests, mountains, flower gardens and beautiful seasides.
